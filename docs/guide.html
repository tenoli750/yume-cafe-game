How To Play

Tap the center brewer to generate a random Lv1, Lv2, or Lv3 drink on an empty board tile.
Drag a drink onto an empty tile to move it around the 5x5 board.
Merge two identical drinks of the same level to upgrade them into the next drink tier.
Tap a customer slot to select who you want to serve next.
Drag a requested drink directly onto the selected customer card to fill that order line.
Customers arrive at random times and leave if their patience timer expires, so keep the queue moving.
